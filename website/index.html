Astryx

Know what's worth shooting before you haul the gear outside. Astryx lays out what's in the sky tonight, when it clears your horizon, and how it'll actually frame in your rig.

  • Plan the night — altitude, transit time, and moon interference for any target, at your exact location.
  • Frame it first — preview field of view for your scope and camera before the target ever rises.
  • Track your sessions — log conditions and outcomes so next time's plan is better than this one.
